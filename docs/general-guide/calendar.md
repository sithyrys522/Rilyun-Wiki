# The Calendar:
There are 372 days a year, with 12 months, 31 days each and 7 days a week. There are 4 seasons, and two moons, one sun. 

- Moons:
    - Small Moon Zylralei [Zihl-Rah-Lee] 31 day cycle
    - Large Moon Jorona [Or-own-uh] 372 day cycle

- Seasons: 
    - Fall - Amberhigh
    - Winter - Frosttide 
    - Spring - Blossomreach
    - Summer - Dayreign
